Welcome to the QIU Final Year Project (FYP) Repository

This system serves as a centralized platform for students, faculty, and researchers of Quest International University (QIU) to store, manage, and access Final Year Projects (FYPs). Designed with innovation and accessibility in mind, the repository is a key resource for fostering collaboration and supporting academic growth.

System Features

  • Submission: Students can easily upload their FYPs for evaluation and archiving.
  • Search: A powerful search tool allows users to find projects based on title, author, subject, or date.
  • Update: Modify or update submitted project information when necessary.
  • Admin Tools: Moderators and supervisors can manage submissions, evaluate projects, and provide feedback.
  • Access Anywhere: The repository is accessible online, ensuring that all users can retrieve information anytime.

Whether you are contributing your own work or exploring the diverse range of projects, this repository aims to enhance the academic experience at QIU by promoting knowledge sharing and collaboration.
